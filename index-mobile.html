BGMI

  BGMI, or Battlegrounds Mobile India, is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game for the Indian market by Krafton. It allows players to compete in intense solo, duo, or squad matches on different maps. BGMI includes cultural elements and language options that resonate with Indian gamers. Despite past regulatory issues, the game remains a favorite among mobile gamers in India due to its engaging and competitive gameplay.

Valo

                  Valorant, known as VALO, is a popular tactical first-person shooter from Riot Games. It combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities in strategic 5v5 matches that emphasize teamwork and communication. The game focuses on attack and defense scenarios, with teams completing objectives. VALO's fast-paced, competitive gameplay has led to a strong esports scene and attracts players with regular updates and events.
